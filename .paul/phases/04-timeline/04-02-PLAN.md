<objective>
## Goal
Add entity linking to events (which characters, places, factions were involved?) and a visual chronology view for scanning the world's timeline at a glance.

## Purpose
Events without entity links are just dates. Linking events to entities creates the web of "who was where when" that makes a world bible useful. The visual chronology gives writers a scannable overview of their world's history.

## Output
- Entity linking on events (many-to-many via entityLinks array)
- "Linked entities" section on event detail page
- Entity link picker in create/edit dialogs
- Visual chronology view on timeline page (toggle between list and timeline)
</objective>
